Bąbel

Logo Design

Bąbel 0 kcal is a brand of flavored sparkling water designed to embody lightness, freshness, and guilt-free pleasure. The minimalist logo and visual identity reflect the product’s character—clean, natural, and energetic. The name “Bąbel” (meaning "bubble" in Polish) captures the sparkling sensation and friendly, playful tone of the brand.

Logo design

Water label series—color-coded flavors with simple illustrations, a bubbly logotype, and a clear layout highlighting “0 kcal.”

Concept Description of labels

A clean and minimal design created to introduce new tea flavour combinations.

Lifestyle imagery

Pure fruit refreshment, zero sugar, naturally inspired.
