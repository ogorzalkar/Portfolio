TieStory

A multifunctional tie box

A tie box with a built-in hanger and a step-by-step guide to tying your tie in two timeless ways.

Product mockup

Cardboard hanger created with die-cutting. It does not require additional cutting thanks to scoring, which facilitates shaping. The design includes spaces for ties – available in versions with two or one slot – as well as a step-by-step instruction for tying a tie in two different ways.
